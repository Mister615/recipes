Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of white bread
 - 2 slices of American cheese (Kraft)
 - 1 oz of butter

Directions:
 1. Heat butter in a pan on medium-high heat.
 2. When butter is fully melted, add both slices of bread to pan.
 3. Once both slices of bread are browned on one side, flip over.
 4. Add cheese to both slices of bread 30 seconds after flipping.
 5. Once cheese is melted, combine both slices of bread where cheese has melted.
 6. Cut completed sandwich in half along the diagonal.
